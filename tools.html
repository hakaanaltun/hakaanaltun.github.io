Tools

Instruments I keep here—one job each, everything computed in your browser.

The Clock

A fullscreen clock, countdown, stopwatch and pomodoro. Large numerals, an analog view—made for classrooms, exams and focused work.

The Page

A blank page to write on. Garamond type, a word count, fullscreen, autosave—and nothing else. Your text stays on your device.

The Draw

A blank page to draw on—mouse, finger or stylus, with pressure. Four inks, undo, autosave and a PNG download; nothing leaves your device.

The Counter

A word counter: words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, reading and speaking time, plus readability—counted live as you type or paste.

The Diff

Two texts compared word by word—every addition and removal, for writers checking drafts. The texts never leave your device.

The Marks

Typography's characters—em dash, curly quotes, thin space and the rest—click any of them to copy it.

The Days

Date arithmetic: days between two dates, the weekday of any date, and where today stands in the year.

The Breath

A guided breathing exercise—box, 4-7-8 and coherent breathing, paced by a slowly swelling circle.

The Noise

White, pink and brown noise, and steady rain—generated live, with a volume dial and a sleep timer.

The Moon

Tonight's moon: the current phase drawn as it hangs in the sky, its illumination and age, and the next full and new moon.

The Season

Where the year stands: the current season, the days until the next solstice or equinox, and the year's progress.

Twilight

İstanbul's sky, kept in real time. Today's sunrise, sunset, civil twilight and day length; the page follows the color of the hour.
